A personal ranker that allows you to rank and sort your own Top 11 for WeTV's Chinese idol survival show Produce Camp 2021 Season 4. It currently has functionality for adding and removing trainees on the table through click, sorting them on the ranking itself through drag & drop, and activating filters that show which contestants have been eliminated or currently hold a top 11 position. It is also possible to save a link of your current ranking to revisit it as the episodes progress.
